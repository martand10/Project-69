ABOUT US

Vision

Empowering young minds in an environment of excellence where sound wisdom, ethical values and academic brilliance is fostered and actualised

Mission

Basing ourselves on the educational system of Don Bosco, we create learning spaces for students to acquire unyielding personal integrity and provide distinctive education to be trailblazers and innovators. The student-centred environment enables every student to recognize their potential and make conscious choices to respond and reform their life and build a more compassionate and just society.

CAMPUS LIFE

Value Based Education

To instil values in children from a young age onwards Don Bosco School of Excellence will be laying a lot of emphasis on Value based education among students.

Maths lab

Our school provides instruction in Mathematics within a supportive learning environment. The teachers present math concepts and practice math-related skills with a focus on the acquisition and mastery of basic skills, and integrate the use of technology beginning in Kindergarten.

Science And Environmental Studies

Our students study Life Science, Earth Science and Physical Science throughout all grade levels.
